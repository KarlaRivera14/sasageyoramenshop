DESIGN DOCUMENT

Purpose of this web site

The purpose of this web site is to provide to current and new customers easy access to the latest updates on Sasageyo's Ramen Shop menu and locations. Its purpose is to also inform customers about Sasageyo's Ramen Shop mission, vision and history. The website shares Sasageyo's favorite recipes and lets customers order via delivery or carryout.

Audience

Sasageyo's Ramen Shop website will be targeted to kids, teenagers, young adults, adults and elders who wish to experience an authentic japanese flavor with ramen bowls, dumplings and desserts.

Message of this website

With this website's creation, Sasageyo Ramen Shop will help expand the knowledge and experience of japanese cuisine. More people will be able to try Japan's most famous dishes.

Goals

  1. Increase Sasageyo's Ramen Shop customers
  2. Provide information to customers
  3. Measure user satisfaction through forms

Navigational Tools

  • Navigation bar
  • Site Map in the footer
  • Drop down menu for "Recipes" in the navigation bar

Organizational Structure

This website will use hierarchical model for its structure.

Outline

  • Home Page:
    • Mission and vision statements
    • Brief history of company
    • Displays three categories of menu
  • Menu Page: Displays all dishes available and a brief description
  • Contact Page:
    • Displays contact information (email, address, phone)
    • Form for customer support
    • Map
  • Order Page: Displays a form based on delivery or carryout option
  • Locations Page:
    • Displays countdown for new locations
    • Current locations
  • Recipes Page:
    • Ramen Recipes
    • Dumplings Recipes
    • Dessert Recipes
    • Links for Firefox and Microsoft home pages
